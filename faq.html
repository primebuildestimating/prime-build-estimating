Frequently Asked Questions

1. Which estimating services do you provide?

We provide professional quantity takeoff and cost estimating services for Residential, Commercial and Industrial construction projects.


2. Which trades do you cover?

We specialize in HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Concrete, Lumber, Site Work, Utilities and all other construction trades.


3. What is your turnaround time?

Most estimates are delivered within 24–48 hours depending on the project size and scope.


4. Which software do you use?

We use industry-standard estimating software such as PlanSwift, Bluebeam Revu and Microsoft Excel.


5. Do you work only in the USA?

Our primary focus is construction estimating services for projects across the United States.


6. How can I request an estimate?

You can contact us through our Contact page or by email at primebuildestimating1@gmail.com.


7. Do you provide estimates for all project sizes?

Yes. We prepare estimates for small residential projects as well as large commercial and industrial developments.
